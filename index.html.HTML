WELCOME TO STRIKERS FOOTBALL CLUB

OUR MISSION:

The strikers football club brings recreational youth soccer to the families of south trinidad. Our goal is to provide the highest quality recreational soccer programme in the south.

LOCATION:

We are currently located at #12 Pine Avenue San Fernando. We are currently accepting coaching applicants for all ages for the 2008/2009 season,if you are interested, please send and email to scffootball@gmail.com
